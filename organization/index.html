# Organization

HUNT Cloud is part of HUNT Research Centre (opens new window) at ISM/MH/NTNU.

  • Ownership
  • Governance
  • Operations

# Ownership

HUNT Cloud is owned by the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (opens new window) (NTNU), Trondheim, Norway.

# Governance

The Director of HUNT Research Centre, Marit Næss (opens new window), is the risk owner for HUNT Cloud with the direct responsibility for our governance.

# Operations

The Director of HUNT Cloud, Oddgeir Lingaas Holmen (opens new window), holds the direct responsibility for our day-to-day operations, including management of information security and privacy.

# Affiliation

HUNT Cloud is operated by HUNT Research Centre (opens new window) which is part of the Department of Public Health and Nursing (opens new window) (ISM) at the Faculty of Medicine and Health Sciences (opens new window) (MH) at the Norwegian University of Science and Technology (opens new window) (NTNU), Trondheim, Norway.
