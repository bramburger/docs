# Certificates

This page list our third-party verified certificates. For each certificate, an independent auditor examines our management, infrastructure, services and operations.

  • ISO 9001
  • ISO 27001

# ISO 9001

ISO 9001:2015 Certificate (pdf)

Our compliance with the international standard in quality management «ISO 9001» is certified by Kiwa, Norway.

# ISO 27001

ISO 27001:2013 Certificate (pdf)

Our compliance with the international standard in information security management «ISO 27001» is certified by Nemko, Norway.

The certification include compliance with controls from «ISO 27017» that covers security for cloud services, and compliance with controls from «ISO 27018» that covers protection of personal identifiable information in public clouds.
