# Certificates

This page list our third-party verified certificates. For each certificate, an independent auditor examines our management, infrastructure, services and operations.

  • ISO 9001
  • ISO 27001

# ISO 9001

ISO 9001:2015 Certificate (opens new window) (pdf)

Our compliance with the international standard in quality management «ISO 9001» is certified by Kiwa, Norway.

# ISO 27001

ISO 27001:2013 Certificate (opens new window) (pdf)

Our compliance with the international standard in information security management «ISO 27001 (opens new window)» is certified by Nemko, Norway.

The certification include compliance with controls from «ISO 27017 (opens new window)» that covers security for cloud services, and compliance with controls from «ISO 27018 (opens new window)» that covers protection of personal identifiable information in public clouds.
